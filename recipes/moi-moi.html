Moi-moi

Description

This is a Nigerian delicious, savory steamed bean pudding made from blended black eyed peas, peppers, onions and spices

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of black eyed peas
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 3 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 large onion

Steps

  1. Boil water in electric kettle and measure 4 cups into pot
  2. Cover the pot and leave water to boil
  3. Lower heat then add in the yam flour into water at a go then stir with turning spoon till it comes together
  4. Add in the reserved cup of water, cover and leave to steam for 1-2 minutes
  5. Stir amala till it's smooth and fluffy
